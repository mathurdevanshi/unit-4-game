Welcome friends to the most wonderous game in the world!

Prove your mathematical abilities through this game through these simple steps:
1. See that box where it says, "Number you are trying to get to?" Well, you are trying to this number.
2. How do you get this number? Good question. You have the help of your four pupper pals down there - Billy Crystal, Crystal Light, Rocky, Chris Rock. By clicking on a crystal (HAHA WHAT A PUN) you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.
3. You win the game by matching your number to the number you are trying to get to. If you go too high, you loose. If you win, congrats! You can now do elementary math.
4. The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it. Each time the game restarts the value of each crystal resets.
